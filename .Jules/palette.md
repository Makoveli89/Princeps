## 2025-02-18 - Streamlit UX Constraints
**Learning:** Streamlit apps differ significantly from standard React/HTML apps. "Accessibility" often means better `help` strings, `placeholders`, and logical layout rather than ARIA labels, which are often abstracted away.
**Action:** When improving Streamlit apps, focus on *content design* (labels, help text, empty states) as the primary accessibility lever.
